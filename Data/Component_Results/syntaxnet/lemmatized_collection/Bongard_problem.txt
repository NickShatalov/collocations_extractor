a bongard problem be a kind of puzzle invent by the russian computer scientist mikhail moiseevich bongard михаил моисеевич бонгард probably in the mids . they were publish in his book on pattern recognition . bongard in the introduction of the book which deal with a number of topic including perceptrons credit the idea in it to a group include m . vaintsvaig v . maksimov and m . smirnov . the idea of a bongard problem be to present two set of relatively simple diagram say a and b . all the diagram from set a have a common factor or attribute which is lack in all the diagram of set b . the problem be to find or to formulate convincingly the common factor . the problem were popularise by their occurrence in the book gdel escher bach by douglas hofstadter himself a composer of bongard problem . bongard problem be also at the heart of the game zendo . many computational architecture have been devise to solve bongard problems the most extensive of which be phaeaco by harry foundalis who leave the field in due to ethical concern regard machine that can pass a human .
