the computational intelligence and machine learning ciml community portal be an international multiuniversity initiative . it primary purpose be to help facilitate a virtual scientific community infrastructure for all those involve with or interest in computational intelligence and machine learn . this include ciml research education and applicationoriented resource reside at the portal and others that are link from the ciml site . the ciml community portal wa create to facilitate an online virtual scientific community wherein anyone interest in ciml can share research obtain resource or simply learn more . the effort is currently lead by jacek zurada principal investigator with rammohan ragade and janusz wojtusiak aid by a team of volunteer researcher from different country . the ultimate goal of the ciml community portal be to accommodate and cater to a broad range of user include expert student the public and outside researcher interest in use ciml method and software tool . each community member and user will be guide through the portal resource and tool base on their respective ciml experience e . expert student outside researcher and goals e . collaboration education . a preliminary version of the communitys portal with limited capability be now operational and available for user . all electronic resource on the portal are peerreviewed to ensure high quality and citeability for literature .
