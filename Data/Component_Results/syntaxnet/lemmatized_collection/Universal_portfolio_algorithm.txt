the universal portfolio algorithm be a portfolio selection algorithm from the field of machine learn and information theory . the algorithm learn adaptively from historical data and maximize the logoptimal growth rate in the long run . it wa introduce by the late stanford university information theorist thomas m . cover . the algorithm rebalances the portfolio at the beginning of each trading period . at the beginning of the first trading period it start with a naive diversification . in the follow trading period the portfolio composition depend on the historical total return of all possible constantrebalanced portfolio .
